## Description

OmicsTrust is a research-use-only evidence audit system for high-dimensional omics. It tests whether a result is interpretable before researchers carry it into expensive validation.

The deterministic engine audits data quality, structural signal, empirical-null calibration, batch and donor risk, technical covariates, stability, reproducibility, failure hierarchy, claim boundaries, and evidence provenance. Evidence Copilot uses GPT-5.6 to translate natural-language research questions into registered workflows and explain deterministic evidence without changing statistics or exposing raw expression matrices or patient rows.

The VANISH PC11 / VasoGate case is a retrospective internal treatment-response hypothesis. It is not a biomarker, treatment recommendation, causal conclusion, or clinical decision tool. Locked independent validation is required.

Founder-reported outreach status: the report has been shared with Professor Anthony Gordon, Professor Tom van der Poll, and Dr David Antcliffe for scientific review. Possible re-analysis has been discussed. This does not imply endorsement, replication, institutional validation, or a commitment to a new trial.

Built with Codex and GPT-5.6. Research Use Only.
